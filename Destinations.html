About

From Mayon Volcano in Albay to the Caramoan Islands of Camarines Sur, the Bicol region is best known for its breathtaking sceneries. Now that travel restrictions are easing up, this destination (which is only an hour’s plane ride away, or 8 to 10 hours if you’re driving or taking the bus from Manila) can offer you beautiful beaches, mountains, and other scenic spots to visit and extreme adventures to try.
